Pie Chart

To create a pie chart in Power BI, you need to follow these steps:

1. Open Power BI Desktop and connect to your data source.
2. Drag and drop the field you want to use for the pie chart into the "Values" section of the "Visualizations" pane.
3. Drag and drop any additional fields you want to use for grouping or slicing the data into the "Legend" or "Details" section of the "Visualizations" pane.
4. Power BI will automatically generate a pie chart based on your selections.
5. Customize the appearance of the pie chart by using the formatting options available in the "Visualizations" pane.
6. Save and publish your report to share it with others.

That's it! You have successfully created a pie chart in Power BI.
